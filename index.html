BETA MOVEMENT
International Student film Festival



Vision

Fostering Cross-Cultural Dialogue

Curating films from diverse backgrounds to encourage meaningful conversations on social, environmental, and urban narratives.

Sparking Collaborative Synergy

Bringing together students, architects, filmmakers, and creative minds to inspire new perspectives and interdisciplinary collaborations.

Redefining Film as a Medium

Challenging conventions by exploring experimental, independent, and student-led cinema that sparks new conversations

A Platform for Untold Stories

Elevating diverse voices, unheard perspectives, and socially relevant themes that inspire change and awareness
